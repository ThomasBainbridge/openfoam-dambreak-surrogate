- Local maximum 0.025 s rolling peak pressure [Pa]

- Area-average pressure impulse [Pa s]
  Model: poly3_ridge with log1p target
  Validation R2: 0.980356
  Validation normalised RMSE: 3.665%

- Maximum-pressure impulse [Pa s]
  Model: knn_distance with log1p target
  Validation R2: 0.967396
  Validation normalised RMSE: 4.149%

- First distributed wetting time [s]
  Model: gaussian_process_matern with raw target
  Validation R2: 0.973518
  Validation normalised RMSE: 5.293%

Interpretation:
  The raw instantaneous peak-pressure quantities were difficult to learn because they are controlled by short-duration transient events. The more physically robust outputs, especially rolling peak pressures, pressure impulses, and distributed wetting time, produced substantially better validation performance on the 60 off-grid validation cases.
